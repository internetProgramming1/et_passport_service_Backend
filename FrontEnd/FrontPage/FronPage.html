BEFORE YOU APPLY

  • ✔️ All applicants should secure an online appointment when applying for a passport
  • ✔️ Confirmed appointment is required
  • ✔️ Applicants are reminded to be on site not earlier than 30 minutes on their selected date and time of appointment at their chosen consular office.
  • ✔️ Personal appearance is required for new applicants.
  • ✔️ Please be warned that dealing with fixers (illegal arrangements) is at your own risk and expense.
  • ✔️ Prepare all the originals and photocopies of the documentary requirements for passport application.
